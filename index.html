✦ BRAND STORY

The MEET
Story

How we are reshaping temporary recruitment for agencies, workers, and the wider community.

Recruitment agencies often face a tough situation. If they do not have enough candidates to fill shifts, they risk losing contracts with their clients. But if they do have workers ready and could not find enough shifts, they risk losing money and trust. Either way, someone or something is left unused — shift or staff.

MEET Resources Limited was created to solve this problem. We have built a secure online platform where agencies can connect and share their unused resources. If one agency has extra staff and another has open shifts, they can work together instead of missing out.

But MEET is not just about agencies. Job seekers can also use the platform to get accredited, upload their documents, complete checks, and get matched with temporary jobs that suit their skills. It is fast, reliable, and built to help everyone move forward.

MEET is here to make temporary recruitment smarter, fairer, and more connected for agencies, workers, and the wider community.
