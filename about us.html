About Us

We effortlessly optimize
temporary recruitment
workforce distribution.

We are revolutionising temporary recruitment with a cutting-edge web platform that streamlines worker and hirer engagement, while optimising agency shift-staff exchanges through a seamless, secure, and user-friendly experience.

Our team is always expanding, but we all work towards one goal: to bridge the gap in recruitment industries in the United Kingdom and make shift‑staff exchange seamlessly possible.

MEET is a recruitment technology business which is at the front of revolutionizing the temporary recruitment sector. Led by a dynamic team with background experiences in I.T, H.R, Customer Service and Sales…
